Accra Chamber of Commerce - Website Plan

1. Site Name

Name: Accra Chamber of Commerce

Reason: The name represents a business hub in Accra, providing a platform for businesses to network, grow, and succeed. It aligns with the organizationâ€™s mission of supporting local businesses and economic growth.

2. Site Purpose

This website will serve as a resource hub for businesses in Accra, Ghana. The site will provide information on local events, networking opportunities, business directories, and resources for entrepreneurs. It will promote economic growth, support local businesses, and create a strong business community.

3. Scenarios

  • How can I become a member of the Accra Chamber of Commerce?
  • Where can I find a list of registered businesses under the Chamber?
  • What upcoming networking events and business conferences are available?

4. Color Schema

The color palette is designed to convey professionalism, trust, and a vibrant business community.

  • light Blue (#0044cc): Used for headers, footer, and call-to-action buttons.
  • Gold (#FFcc00): Used for accents, highlights, and icons.
  • White (#FFFFFF): Used for backgrounds, navigations and content areas.
  • black (#000000): Used for text and secondary information.

5. Typography

The website will use a clean, modern font combination for readability and professionalism.

  • Montserrat (Headings): Used for all headings and navigation items.
  • Open Sans (Body Text): Used for paragraph content, ensuring readability.
  • Roboto (Buttons & Labels): Used for buttons and interactive elements.
